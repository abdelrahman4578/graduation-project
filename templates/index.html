ABSTRACT

Pneumonia is a highly fatal disease that kills 50,000 Americans each year. Pneumonia is a serious illness that affects children under the age of five and the elderly over the age of 65. Each year, the United States spends billions of dollars fighting pneumonia-related diseases. In the treatment of pneumonia-related diseases, early detection and intervention is crucial. We propose a deep learning approach based on CNN convolutional neural networks to identify and classify cases of pneumonia from chest X-ray images because it is one of the simplest and cheapest methods for diagnosing pneumonia.

Thus, the development of automated monitoring system in the evaluation of pneumonia has gained more importance in treating or curing the disease in the early stages. Deep learning algorithms such as CNN (Convolutional Neural Network) have gained a lot of attention for disease classification.
